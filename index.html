Industries We Serve

Most of our clients are in industries where confidentiality is contractual — healthcare, legal, finance, government. They trust us partly because we take that seriously.

What we can tell you: the strategy was sound, the creative was sharp, and the numbers moved. Below are five anonymised accounts of engagements that represent the kind of work we do and the outcomes we deliver.

Healthcare / GCC

The situation: A multi-site hospital group had strong clinical standing but poor visibility, losing patients to more visible competitors.
What we did: Full brand audit, new website, and targeted paid media across three channels for patients and referring physicians.
What changed: New online appointment requests surged from previously inactive channels, aligning digital presence with their high standard of care.

Legal Services / EU

The situation: A mid-size commercial law firm had no inbound pipeline. Outdated digital presence meant losing clients to competitors perceived as more credible.
What we did: Brand refresh, website overhaul, and a LinkedIn content strategy focused on genuine thought leadership.
What changed: Generated first-ever inbound commercial enquiries and significantly improved market perception and referral quality.

Finance / Global

The situation: A traditional wealth management firm struggled to attract next-gen clients due to a legacy brand disconnected from digital expectations.
What we did: Strategic rebranding and a modern content strategy centered on transparency and financial agility.
What changed: Lowered the average age of new clients by 15 years without alienating their trusted legacy client base.

Insurance / EU & GCC

The situation: A regional insurance provider faced massive competitors with indistinguishable branding and messaging.
What we did: Differentiation strategy, creative identity, performance marketing, and a targeted lead capture system.
What changed: Inbound lead volume and quality improved within two months, and the brand established a distinct regional presence.

Hospitality / GCC

The situation: A boutique hotel group had high website traffic but low conversions, and social media that failed to reflect their premium experience.
What we did: Revamped content strategy, social management, art direction, and UX improvements to the booking flow.
What changed: Direct bookings increased and the properties gained a distinct visual identity that stood out from chain competitors.

FAQ — methodology & engagement

Why don't you publish a full portfolio?

Most of our clients are in industries where confidentiality is contractual. We build a tailored capabilities preview specific to your sector and challenges, instead of sending a generic deck.

What kind of organisations do you work with?

We partner with established organisations in sectors where trust and credibility are essential. Our clients need an agency that delivers measurable outcomes, not just impressive presentations.

How do you structure engagements?

We offer project-based, retainer, or hybrid models based on what makes sense for your organisation. Every relationship begins with a discovery session to align on your actual needs.

Where do you operate?

We work with clients across Europe, the United States, and the Gulf Cooperation Council. Our distributed team understands the specific digital landscape and cultural context of each territory.

What makes Respace different from other agencies?

We tie our success directly to yours by building strategy, creative, and performance as one integrated system. We also work with a deliberately small number of clients to ensure focused attention.

What does the first conversation look like?

It's an honest, no-pressure discussion about your business and goals. We'll tell you if there's a fit and show you exactly what we'd do to help.

How do you measure performance?

We track the metrics that matter to your business, such as pipeline, qualified enquiries, and revenue. We focus on real commercial outcomes rather than vanity numbers.
